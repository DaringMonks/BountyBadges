Edumonk Chief - Execution & Strategy Management

Inspiring Young Minds With Magical Imaginations !

A social young mind lost in connecting different strings of Learning Activities and Employment prospects around the globe to make it uniform and accessible to all. Being brought up in a rural middle class family with a limited set of defined learning paths with a blissful reward structure itches him the need to bring uniform rewarding and evaluation strategy change through optimization and Innovations of processes within this space. Having a strong belief in people controlled decentralized economy he believes this organisation to be like a ship which he wishes to steer moving on his own chariot of dreams connecting the complimentary dots by moving through diverse geographies and interacting with education based communities of different ethnicities along the journey. Devoting and dedicating himself to his vision and dreams has brought him over in a state where most of his time is spent analyzing and optimizing different skill based learning and employability framework globally.

According to him this world is a place where we all of us are sent to perform our roles moving on our own learning path and reward structure for different actions and reactions. If this path is disturbed in anyway, the whole resonance of processes and stability across the globe would collapse and ultimately bring adverse results which we can easily observe around us no matter where we live and where we belong. The same law of nature follows with the Education and Employment merging strategies. We have already made the whole system fragmented and broken with no complete resonance structure where individuals can share a common identity and steer forward with different thoughts devising their own learning path. Given the opportunity of meeting different people on the same journey can make a very large knowledge sharing funnel united on a common ground of getting skilled and finding opportunities with a uniform and decentralized rewarding structure. Inspiring young minds through Magical Imaginations and bringing out the ancient culture in a modern way with the best technology available changing the rigid training structure into self paced learning for every individual on this earth is the ultimate goal he wishes to accomplish and achieve in his future endeavours.
