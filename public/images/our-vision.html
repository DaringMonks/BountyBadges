Our Vision

Inspiring Young Leaders Through Magical Imaginations and Innovations Our diverse team with different professional and having varied interests within us is on a common goal of bringing educational opportunities to all mapping different learning curriculum with local and global employability framework on a blockchain based decentralised non-profit structure. With this foundation we wish to bring phenomenal change in skill development and philanthropic giving through different reward based learning grants and scholarship distribution models.
