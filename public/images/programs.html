Program Overview

All our programs come with a 45 day free training during which the individuals are set to move on their own learning path tweaked slightly with our imaginations to bring together a positive change in the society. After imparting them the skills they need and analyzing their performance during the live training during this period, they will be awarded fellowship within the organization which will help them further accompolish their goals either building unique profitable ventures for them or mapping them with prospective employment opportunities. Further moving on the road we wish to create Edumonk as a virtual entity which will be able to educate the people in a fun way with the help of a virtual environment created using Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality teleporting the individuals and giving training in a fun way by giving them the live experience of the whole world at the comfort of their place.
