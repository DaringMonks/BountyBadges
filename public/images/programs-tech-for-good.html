TECH FOR GOOD

TECH FOR GOOD IS ONE OF OUR PROGRAM IN WHICH INDIVIDUALS WITH DIFFERENT TECH SKILLS JOIN HANDS TO MAKE EDUMONK THE BEST AND SUSTAINABLE PLATFORM




This program will be mainly responsible for making dreamdiaries of the needy people and bringing it forward in the light of mainstream audience with the use of Social Media and Crowdfunding Campaigns mixed with short film making and Blog writing. The community would majorly consist of writers, videomakers and social media junkies who love promoting noble causes through their imaginations and skills altogether.
